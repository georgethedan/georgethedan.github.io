About

  • What is DOTT?

    DOTT is a dairy and timetable organiser app. It was made by George Dan

  • Who is it for?

    Currently, DOTT has been developed for Year 7 students at Canberra Grammar School, but can be adapted and used by a variety of students.

  • What is under the hood?

    Under the hood, DOTT uses Chocolate Chip UI, Javascript and CSS to deliver a mobile application for smartphone and tablet devices
